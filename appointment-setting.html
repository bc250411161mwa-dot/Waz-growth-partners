Appointment Setting

Structured qualification and calendar conversion for higher close-ready meetings.

What This Service Solves

Replies do not equal revenue. Our appointment setting team operates as an extension of your sales motion, qualifying buyer intent, validating fit, and securing meetings with proper context. This protects your sales capacity and ensures your team spends time only on opportunities with real probability.

Core Deliverables
  • Reply triage and lead scoring framework
  • Qualification script mapped to your deal criteria
  • Calendar booking and confirmation workflow
  • Meeting briefs for your closers before every call
Business Impact
  • Higher show rates with better expectation-setting
  • Lower time spent on non-fit accounts
  • Improved conversion from intro call to next step
  • Cleaner pipeline with standardized qualification data

Our 3-Step Process

1Qualification Mapping: We define qualification thresholds, objection routes, and escalation paths aligned with your sales model.

2Conversation Management: Our setters manage ongoing dialogue, answer first-level questions, and position next-step value clearly.

3Booking and Handoff: We secure call slots, confirm attendance, and pass complete context to your AE or founder before the meeting.
